About Our Team

We are Esafe, a team that aims to encourage password safety and usability. This page introduces our team, mission, and how to reach us.

Mission

Deliver a simple, secure experience, showing how long it would take for your password to be cracked!

Core values

Trust & Integrity — We never store, transmit, or save your passwords. Your security is our fundamental promise, and we maintain absolute transparency in how our tool functions.
User Safety First — Every design decision prioritises your digital safety, from client-side only processing to clear data handling policies. Your security isn't an afterthought, it's our foundation.
Accessible Security — We break down complex cybersecurity concepts into simple, visual feedback that anyone can understand, regardless of their technical background.
